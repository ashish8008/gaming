Forza Horizon 5 is an open-world racing game developed by Playground Games and published by Xbox Game Studios. Released on November 9, 2021, it is the fifth installment in the Forza Horizon series and is available on Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, and PC.

Gameplay & Features

  1. Open-World Mexico: The game is set in a vast, highly detailed representation of Mexico, featuring diverse environments such as jungles, deserts, beaches, cities, and even an active volcano.
  2. Dynamic Weather & Seasons: The game includes a changing climate system, affecting race conditions with seasonal variations like dust storms and tropical rain.
  3. Huge Car Collection: Over 600 cars from brands like Ferrari, Lamborghini, Porsche, and more, each with detailed customization options.
  4. Events & Modes: Includes races, stunt challenges, off-road events, and an improved Horizon Arcade for multiplayer minigames.
  5. Multiplayer & Customization: Features an online open world, allowing players to explore, race, and interact with others through the Horizon Life mode.

Game Setting & Map

The game is set in Mexico, featuring the largest and most diverse open-world map in the series. The environment includes:


  1. Dense jungles 🌳
  2. Sandy deserts 🏜️
  3. Lush farmlands 🌾
  4. Bustling cities 🏙️
  5. Long highways 🛣️
  6. Stunning coastlines & beaches 🏝️
  7. The map is 50% larger than Forza Horizon 4’s Britain, making it the most expansive world in the series.


Multiplayer & Online Features

  1. Horizon Life – Open-world online mode where players interact with real players.
  2. >Convoys – Team up with friends for free-roam adventures or races.
  3. Forza Link – An AI-driven system that helps you connect with players who share similar interests.
