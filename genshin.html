Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game (ARPG) developed and published by HoYoverse (formerly miHoYo). Released in September 2020, it is available on PC, PlayStation 4 & 5, iOS, Android, and later announced for Nintendo Switch.

Gameplay & Features:

Open-World Exploration: Players can freely explore the fantasy world of Teyvat, climbing, swimming, and gliding.

Elemental Combat System: The game features seven elements—Anemo (Wind), Geo (Earth), Electro (Lightning), Dendro (Nature), Hydro (Water), Pyro (Fire), and Cryo (Ice)—which interact in combat.

Gacha System: Characters and weapons are obtained through a gacha-based system (Wishes).

Multiple Playable Characters: Each character has unique abilities, weapons, and elemental affiliations.

Story & Lore: Players follow the journey of the Traveler, searching for their lost sibling while uncovering Teyvat’s mysteries.

Multiplayer Mode: Co-op mode allows up to four players to explore and complete challenges together.


Teyvat & Regions:

  1. Mondstadt (Anemo) – Inspired by medieval European aesthetics.
  2. Liyue (Geo) – Based on Chinese landscapes and culture.
  3. Inazuma (Electro) – Inspired by feudal Japan.
  4. Sumeru (Dendro) – A desert and rainforest region based on Middle Eastern and South Asian influences.
  5. Fontaine (Hydro) – A steampunk, water-themed region inspired by France.
  6. Natlan (Pyro) – Yet to be released (expected to have tribal and volcanic themes).
  7. Snezhnaya (Cryo) – The home of the Fatui and ruled by the Tsaritsa.
