Battel Ground Mobile India

Battlegrounds Mobile India (BGMI) is an online multiplayer battle royale game developed by Krafton for Indian gamers. It is the rebranded version of PUBG Mobile, which was banned in India in 2020 due to security concerns. BGMI was officially launched on July 2, 2021, for Android and later for iOS users.
